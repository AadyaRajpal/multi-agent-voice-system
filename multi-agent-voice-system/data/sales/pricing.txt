Product Pricing and Plans

Our product is available in three tiers designed to meet different business needs:

Basic Plan - $49/month
The Basic plan is perfect for small teams and individual users. It includes all core features needed to get started: project management tools, basic reporting, up to 5 team members, 10GB storage, and email support during business hours. This plan is billed monthly with no long-term commitment required.

Pro Plan - $99/month
Our most popular plan, the Pro tier, is designed for growing businesses. It includes everything in Basic plus: unlimited team members, 100GB storage, priority support with 2-hour response time, advanced analytics and custom reports, API access for integrations, and custom branding options. Annual billing is available with a 20% discount (equivalent to 2 months free).

Enterprise Plan - Custom Pricing
For large organizations with specific needs, our Enterprise plan offers: unlimited storage, dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone support, custom integrations and workflows, SSO and advanced security features, SLA guarantees, and on-premise deployment options. Contact our sales team for a customized quote based on your requirements.
